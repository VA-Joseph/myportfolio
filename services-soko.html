Services Soko

Overview: A market platform connecting clients to freelancers and groups offering services across various sectors — from manual work to creative and white-collar tasks.

Key Features:

  • Service listing and searching by category
  • Freelancer and contractor profiles
  • Secure task/project-based hiring
  • Rating and review system for service providers

Technologies Used:

  • Web App Platform
  • Secure Payment Integration (future plan)
  • Service Discovery Algorithms
  • Task Management System
